Social Branch

Welcome to Social branch! We serve as the bridge between all the branches and focus on creating a community through fun, club-wide events. Some of our most popular events include dumpling making, mahjong nights, game socials, food fundraisers, and various other activities like gingerbread-making during Christmas season.
